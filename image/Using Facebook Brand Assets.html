Talking About Facebook

in Web, Print or Broadcast

Sometimes you may need to refer to Facebook to discuss it, describe your presence on Facebook, display your Facebook web address, indicate that your product is integrated with Facebook, or describe your products or services as they relate to Facebook.

Learn more in our Do’s and Don’ts section.

Do
Capitalize the word “Facebook”, except when it’s part of a web address
Do
Display the word “Facebook” in the same font size and style as the content surrounding it
Don’t
Use the Facebook logo in place of the word “Facebook”
Don’t
Pluralize the Facebook trademark, use it as a verb or abbreviate it
